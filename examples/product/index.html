About Dreams in Transit

Dreams in Transit is a collection of flash fiction, or very short stories, written by Irene P. Smith and published in 2013. Some of these stories were published on the web, but many were created specifically for the collection.

Genres range from Mainstream to Science Fiction and Fantasy. There's even a little touch of Romance. Read about aliens, vampires, and even some real people.
