What is image slicing? It means dividing an image into smaller images. Why would you want to do that? For one thing, it is one way to make an image that links to several different places.

As an example of image slicing, the above image was sliced into nine equal pieces and the center image was linked to the first page of my portfolio. Go ahead and click it if you want to. The link will open in a separate window so you won't lose your place. I have also make a copy of this page with the borders turned on so you can see how the image was divided. Take a look at lesson16b.html to see what I'm talking about.
