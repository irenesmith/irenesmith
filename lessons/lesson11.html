Notice that although only two background tiles were used on this page, it is more interesting than it would normally be because of the three dimensional quality added by the nested tables. Looks nice, don't you think? The only thing I did differently is that all of my tables are defined with a width of 100% and a height of 100% to make them fill the page as much as possible. The spacing between the layers is controlled by changing the cellpadding value instead of the size of the table. This gives me equal borders all the way around the table.
