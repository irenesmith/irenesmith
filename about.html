Who am I?

I am a wife, a mother, and my official title at work is Firefox Developer Content Manager.

In the past I have sold advertising, worked as a Secretary and an Office Manager. I have also been a technical writer, a programmer, a teacher, and even a Travel Agent. I had a very hard time deciding what I wanted to be when I grow up. And, since I haven't really grown up yet, I have plenty of time, right?

We all have multiple personalities.

There is the you that hangs out with your friends, and the one that goes to work every day. You might behave diferently around your family than you do when you're out with your friends. I am no different.

Writer

There is the part of me that is a writer. By day, I write about programming and web design. But in my heart of hearts, I am a story teller. I like writing almost as much as I like talking... and I LOVE to talk.

Just try to stop me... I dare you.

Programmer

Then there is the programmer. I became a programmer because I wanted to understand how computers work. Okay, the truth is that I became a programmer because I couldn't afford a disk drive. Then I discovered that it was fun! I still think programming is fun and love learning new ways languages, algorithms, and design patterns.

Web Designer

Web design has elements of programming and of writing. There is also creative design. I've been making websites from front-end to back since 1996. It started out as a hobby and now I'm getting the opportunity to teach others how design, develop, and code websites as part of my contract at Mozilla.

Photographer

Photography? Yes. My father taught me to use a camera when I was a little girl. Now, with digital photography, I can take as many photographs as I want without worrrying about buying film or paying to have them developed.

The whole package...

If you want to know more about my career, take a look at my resume or check out my "other" site, the one for my non-technical writing.
