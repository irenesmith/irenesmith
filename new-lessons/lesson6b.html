Lesson 6 asks us to create a top border. This is the second of the two methods we were asked to create. The stylesheet for this page defines the heading as tiling only in the horizontal direction.

This design is much boxier than the fancy version but I like it too. I probably wouldn't have tried creating a top border because of the disasterous results I had the last time I created one (the old fashioned way) by trying to make it longer than I'd ever want the page to go. No matter how long you make it, there always seems to come a time when your text is so long you get a second border at the bottom of the page. This way you don't have to worry about that.
