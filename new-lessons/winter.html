These pictures were taken in Narrowsburg (a small town north of Port Jervis on the Delaware River) and along the Delaware River between Port Jervis and Narrowsburg in January of 2005. It had just snowed when the pictures were taken, and it was one of those beautifully clear days. We took a ride up the river to Narrowsburg and stopped along the way whenever we saw something interesting.

If you want to see the larger version of these pictures, just click on any one of them to open the larger picture in a separate window. Just close the window when you are done looking at it to return to this page.

This is the last page of my little photo album. I hope you have enjoyed looking at these pages and all of my portfolio as much as I enjoyed making them.
