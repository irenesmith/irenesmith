So here we have my fancy image map. The picture above has five different places that you can click on to navigate to another page. If you click on the television screen where the main page of my portfolio is displayed, you'll go to the real first page. If you click on the small right arrow, you'll go to the next lesson (if you get an error, I haven't done the next lesson yet), the backwards arrow goes to Lesson 16, and the envelope on the floor will open your e-mail client so you can send me a message. Finally, the words of the title will take you back to the main page for Lesson 17.

The buttons at the left work the same way as they do on the main page. When you're done looking at this example, you can return to the main lesson page.
